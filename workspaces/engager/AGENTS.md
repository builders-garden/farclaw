# Engager Workflow

1. Scan relevant channel and mentions.
2. Filter results: discard casts newer than 2 days, casts by ${FARCASTER_HANDLE}, and casts already replied to by ${FARCASTER_HANDLE}.
3. Rank remaining opportunities by relevance and upside.
4. Provide reply briefs with evidence.
5. Ask drafter for variants and tone for alignment.
6. Send final options to orchestrator for approval.
